The Power of Matrices

Transformers process language not as words, but as math. Specifically, matrix operations. Seeing these matrices interact reveals how the model "understands" context.

üåü ELI12: The Party Analogy

Imagine you are at a crowded party. You are a word in a sentence.

  • Query ($Q$): What you are looking for (e.g., "I'm looking for someone who likes video games").
  • Key ($K$): What you advertise about yourself (e.g., "I have a badge that says 'Gamer'").
  • Value ($V$): The actual information you share (e.g., "Here is my Steam ID").

Self-Attention is just you scanning the room, matching your Query with everyone's Key. If they match well, you pay attention to their Value!
