Cross-Attention

Bridging the Encoder and Decoder.

The Bridge

In sequence-to-sequence models (like translation), the Decoder needs to look at the Encoder's output. This is Cross-Attention.

Key concept: The Queries come from the Decoder (what I'm translating now), but the Keys and Values come from the Encoder (the original sentence).

Decoder (Target)

Generates Query ($Q$)

Encoder (Source)

Provides Key ($K^T$) & Value ($V$)
